How to Use My HandFan

Video of My Fan in Use

Using my device is really easy. To turn it on, just push the joystick forward. This will start the device and get it running.
When you're done and want to stop it, pull the joystick backward, and the device will turn off. There's also a small red light on the top of the device that helps you know if it's on or off.
When the red light is on, that means the device is running. If the light is off, the device is not on. It’s a simple way to make sure you always know what’s going on with the device. One of the challenges I faced whilst making my device was the wires would always fall out.
I even tried to hold down the fan with tape but when I was trying to fix the wires,the tape pulled the whole wires out. So I had to rewire the whole thing.
Another challenge was when I was making sure the joystick, lightbulb and DC motor were connected to my project, the DC motor was unable to connect at the bottom. So I had to use tape instead. Something I would use differently would be, possibly using a remote,
or using a different type of sticky tack to connecct my fan.
